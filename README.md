# 🛢️ Retail Sales SQL Analytics (Task 3)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project contains advanced SQL queries written in MySQL Workbench to extract deep business insights from the cleaned retail dataset. The queries analyze product performance, temporal sales trends, marketing channel values, and fulfillment metrics.

---

## 🔍 Core SQL Queries & Business Insights

### 1. Revenue & Product Performance
* **Top Revenue Generator:** Identified the single highest revenue-generating product by aggregating total sales and filtering for the top result.
* **Volume Analysis:** Tracked the exact total quantity sold for every product category to measure demand.

### 2. Time-Series Sales Trends
* **Temporal Tracking:** Segmented sales metrics across different years and months to isolate key periods of growth and performance seasonality.

### 3. Marketing & Customer Analytics
* **Channel Value:** Evaluated traffic sources (`Instagram`, `Facebook`, `Email`, etc.) to find out which channel brings in the highest combined order volume and total revenue.
* **Top Customer:** Identified the highest-spending consumer profile based on product acquisition volumes.

### 4. Operational & Fulfillment Health
* **Order Status Percentages:** Calculated the precise percentage rates for **Cancelled**, **Returned**, and **Delivered** shipments to evaluate supply chain efficiency.
